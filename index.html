Welcome to Krisan Flower

Chrysanthemums or Krisan flower are flowers that symbolize happiness and love. Chrysanthemum flowers come from China, with a height of 60 cm to 90 cm. Chrysanthemum flowers have more than 40 different species, some of which are Pillow Chrysanthemums, Quilled Bloms, Spider Bloms, Chrysanthemum Inodorum, Chrysanthemum Carinatum, and Chrysanthemum Pompoms. Apart from being used as a beautiful decoration, chrysanthemum flowers can also be used for medical treatments such as relieving sore throats and helping overcome digestive disorders.

Symbol Krisan Flower

The chrysanthemum, or krisan, originated in ancient China over 2,500 years ago as a medicinal herb and symbol of nobility. It spread to Japan in the 8th century, becoming a national emblem of the imperial family. Introduced to Europe in the 17th century, it is now cherished worldwide, symbolizing happiness in the East and remembrance in the West.
